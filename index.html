Department of Mathematics and Statistics at UMBC will hold the 16th Annual Probability and Statistics Day at UMBC during May 9th & 10th, 2025. The event consists of a Full day conference on Friday and Saturday. Probability and Statistics Day at UMBC is open to all from all academic institutions, government agencies, and private industries.

Registration is required!
