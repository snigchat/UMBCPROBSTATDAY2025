Directions & Map

From the North, take I-95 to Route 166 (Exit 47B, Catonsville) or take I-83 to the Baltimore Beltway (I-695 West) and then take Exit 12C (Wilkens Avenue, West); follow the signs to UMBC.

From the South, take I-95 to Route 166 (Exit 47B, Catonsville); follow the signs to UMBC.


Parking is available in the Commons Drive Garage (closer to the Public Policy building) or the Administration Drive Garage (closer to the Information Technology/Engineering building). Parking permits for Friday will be made available to non-students; parking on Saturday (including metered parking) is free.
